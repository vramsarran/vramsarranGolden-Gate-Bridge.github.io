Biking and Walking the Golden Gate Bridge
Cyclists have free access to the East and West sidewalks 24 hours a day. Pedestrians can walk on the East sidewalk facing San Francisco from 5 a.m. to 6:30 p.m. Walk or bike to the Golden Gate National Parks with picturesque trails and vista points on either end.
